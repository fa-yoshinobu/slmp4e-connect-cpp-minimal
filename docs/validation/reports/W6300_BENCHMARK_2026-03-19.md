## 3. Conclusion

The `slmp::SlmpClient` successfully handles both 4E and 3E frames on the RP2350 platform. The 3E frame reduces the header overhead by 4 bytes as expected. While the throughput is lower than the ESP32-based Atom Matrix (which achieved 110 req/sec in a different network environment), the consistency of the response times (80-82ms) indicates a reliable transport layer.
